ABOUT MEZAN BEVERAGES

Cola NEXT is one of the largest beverage brands of Pakistan. It is known for its strong, fizzy and distinguishing flavors.

Most Important To Us is Good Taste

Our company, The Paracha Textile Mills Limited was established in the year 1951-52. The foundation stone was laid down by the excellency of Khawaja Nazimuddin, the then Governor General of Pakistan. In 1983, the current management took over the company. After taking over the company, the board of directors carried their vision of edible oil market being a respectable, fairly new and developing market in Pakistan. The Ghee Unit Batch refinery was established and started manufacturing different brands of Ghee and Cooking Oil.

Now Mezan Group is the proud producer of Mezan Oil &Banaspati, Olive Oil (Olivola), Mezan Tea and provides office space in the state of the art Mezan Tower handled by Mezan Property Wing.

Balanced and wholesome food, invigorating taste, health life style, contemporary living and nature friendly environment for everyone. Mezan with its roots planted deep in the culture and society of Pakistan takes pride in being a socially responsible company.
